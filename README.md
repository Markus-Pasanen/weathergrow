## Weathergrow - a school project

Weathergrow is a mobile virtual pet plant game for Android that brings your environment to life by connecting your in-game plant's wellbeing to real-world weather conditions. Designed for casual gamers who want a low-maintenance, personal, and responsive experience, Weathergrow makes daily care feel meaningful and unique to every player.

### Key Features

- **Real Weather Integration:** Your virtual plant reacts to your local weather using a weather API. Sunny days dry it out, rain gives it a boost, and each plant species responds differently to your actual climate.
- **Personal Connection:** No two players have the same experience—your plant's health and growth are tied directly to your real-world location and weather, making your care routine genuinely reflect where you live.
- **Low-Maintenance Gameplay:** Perfect for players who enjoy casual games that don't require constant attention, but still want something that feels alive and rewarding.
- **Variety of Plant Species:** Choose from a range of plant species, each with unique responses to weather and care routines.
- **Decorative Customization:** Unlock and purchase decorative pots and accessories to personalize your plant's environment.

### Monetization

- **Free-to-Play:** The core game is free for everyone.
- **Premium Content:** Optional purchases include premium plant species, decorative pots, and time-skip items for impatient players.
- **Rewarded Ads:** Watch video ads for small in-game currency boosts.

### Why Weathergrow?

Weathergrow offers a fresh take on virtual pet games by making your plant's wellbeing truly responsive to your real-world environment. Whether it's a rainy week or a heatwave, your plant's needs and growth will reflect your local weather, making every player's journey unique and personal.
